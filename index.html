Outpatient Delirium Monitoring Portal

Prototype for caregiver-reported delirium screening, longitudinal monitoring, and clinician review. This keeps the original family guide structure, but turns it into a workflow tool with saved check-ins, a dummy patient login, and a doctor dashboard.

Prototype only. No real PHI. Built for discussion with Dr. Nimit Agarwal.
